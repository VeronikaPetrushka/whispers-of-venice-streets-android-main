Title: Whispers of Venice Streets

Description: 

Whispering Streets of Venice is your personal guide to help you uncover the hidden soul of Venice. Step outside the tourist crowds and discover a curated collection of historic sites, cultural gems, romantic corners and authentic local attractions – all through the voice of your virtual guide.

Designed for explorers, dreamers and culture seekers, the app includes:

- Expertly recommended places to visit
- A personal list of saved favourites
- An interactive map, perfect for offline viewing
- A cultural blog

Let Venice reveal itself in whispers – one quiet alley, one breathtaking view, one untold story at a time.
